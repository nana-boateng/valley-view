Recreational Information

There are many fitness and recreational groups and classes available in Lumsden including:

  • Yoga
  • Martial Arts (Kempo & Karate)
  • Fitness Clubs
  • Dance (Preschool, Tap, Jazz, Acrobatics, Ballet)
  • Community Adult Band
  • Swim Programs
  • Hockey
  • Figure Skating
  • Groomed Snowmobiling Trails
  • Curling
  • Local library that offers programs for all ages

There are various attractions nearby including; Numerous beaches on Last Mountain Lake, Dallas Valley Ranch Camp, St. Michaels Retreat House, Deer Valley Golf Course, Lumsden Beach Camp, Last Mountain House, Trans Canada Trail as well as local artists and craftspeople.
